FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently asked questions about stays with children, the use of parking, etc. are answered here. Please refer to this page when making a reservation.
Q. Is there an Internet connection in guestrooms?
A wired LAN Internet connection is available free of charge in all rooms.
Please feel free to ask at the front desk to borrow a LAN cable.

Outlook or other mail systems cannot be used for security reasons.
Please use Yahoo, Hotmail or other Web-based mail services.

Q. Can I request a room on the Nakajima Park side?
Requests for rooms on the Nakajima Park side are accepted for the following room types:

  • - Hollywood Large Twin
  • - Superior Large Twin
  • - Large Twin
  • - Superior Queen-double
  • - Superior Double
  • - Superior Deluxe Single
  • - Universal Room
  • - Second House
  • - Spa Home
  • - Bride's Double
  • - Royal Twin

Please note that requests for rooms on the Nakajima Park side can be made only for the above room types listed in the accommodation plans and not for other room types.

Q. Can I request a room on an upper floor?
The guestroom floors are on the 5th to 10th floors and the rooms on each floor are as follows:
"Park in Park" on the 10th floor
"Superior" on the 7th to 9th floors
Standard "Single" and "Twin" on the 5th and 6th floors
If you wish to stay on an upper floor, please reserve a room on the "Park in Park" or "Superior" floors.

Q. Is there a parking area?
The parking area for approximately 220 cars at the front of the hotel can be used without a reservation.

Long-stay guests can use parking without worry as the fee for one car is ¥1,030 regardless of the period of stay.

The parking fee for minibuses, camper vans and other large vehicles is ¥5,400 per night (reservation required).
Please contact the reservation desk (011-511-3135).

Q. Can guestroom windows be opened? What kind of air conditioning is used?
The hotel does not provide air conditioning systems for individual rooms, but guests can adjust cooling levels in summer and heating levels in winter. Guests can also have guestroom windows opened by requesting staff to do so at the front desk or by asking a room clerk. Please feel free to ask if you wish to adjust the room temperature or humidity by letting outside air in.

Q. Can I request a non-smoking room?
Different types of non-smoking rooms are available. Please choose a room designated as "non-smoking" at the time of reservation.
If all non-smoking rooms are booked, deodorized smoking rooms may be offered instead.
All rooms on the special floor are smoking rooms and are deodorized for non-smoking guests.

Q. Can I stay with children?
You can search online for rooms available for guests with children by changing the number of persons or other search conditions.

Preschool children sharing beds with parents can stay free of charge. Please include them as infants (without meals or futon) in the reservation.

Charges for infants (with meals and futon), infants (with futon) and elementary school children are the same as those for adults.

There are no charges for elementary school children sharing beds with parents (meals are provided for a fee, separate booking necessary).

Please include them as infants (without meals or futon) in the reservation.

Q. Are there amenities for children?
There are no amenities especially for children.

Toothbrushes, towels and other items will be provided for the number of guests. As there are only adult-sized one-piece dressing gowns, room wear for children must be prepared by guests.

Q. Can I check out later?
Late checkout may be arranged for an additional fee although it is subject to the following day's reservation status. The amount of the additional fee varies by room type and the checkout time. Please contact the hotel if you wish to use this service.

Q. Can I arrive earlier than the check-in time?
Although the check-in time is from 2:00 p.m., parking can be used at no additional charge before the check-in time.

Please ask at the front desk if you wish to leave your luggage at the hotel prior to the check-in time.

Q. What items are available for loan?
Humidifiers, trouser pressers, LAN cables, hair tonic, hair liquid, hair lotion, sewing sets, rental PCs (¥1,000/day), baby cribs (¥2,000/day), buckwheat hull pillows and other items are available. Please make a booking in advance as some items are limited in number.
